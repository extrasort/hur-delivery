# Legal Documents for Hur Delivery

This folder contains the legal documents required for the Hur Delivery app, including versions in both English and Arabic.

## Documents Included

### 1. Privacy Policy
- **English**: `privacy_policy_en.md`
- **Arabic**: `privacy_policy_ar.md`

### 2. Terms and Conditions
- **English**: `terms_and_conditions_en.md`
- **Arabic**: `terms_and_conditions_ar.md`

## Google Play Store Requirements

These documents are formatted to meet Google Play Store requirements and must be uploaded when submitting the app.

### Upload Instructions

1. **Privacy Policy URL**: 
   - Host these documents on a publicly accessible website
   - Enter the URL in Google Play Console → App Content → Privacy Policy
   - Example: `https://yourwebsite.com/privacy-policy`

2. **Terms of Service**:
   - Host on your website
   - Link from within the app and Play Store listing

## Important Notes

### Before Publishing

1. **Update Contact Information**:
   - Replace `privacy@hurdelivery.com` with your actual email
   - Replace `support@hurdelivery.com` with your support email
   - Update phone number `+964 771 234 567` to actual support number
   - Add physical business address

2. **Review Legal Requirements**:
   - Have documents reviewed by legal counsel
   - Ensure compliance with Iraqi laws
   - Verify Google Play Store requirements are met

3. **Hosting Options**:
   - Create a simple website (GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc.)
   - Use a dedicated privacy policy hosting service
   - Include links in app settings

### Document Maintenance

- Review and update annually
- Update "Last Updated" date when making changes
- Notify users of significant changes via app notifications
- Keep previous versions for legal records

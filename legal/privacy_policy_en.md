# Privacy Policy for Hur Delivery

**Last Updated: October 7, 2025**

## Introduction

Hur Delivery ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register and use the App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Contact Information**: Phone number, name, and store name (for merchants)
- **Account Information**: User ID, account type (merchant, driver, or customer), verification status
- **Profile Information**: Profile photo (optional), business address, store details
- **Financial Information**: Delivery fees, order amounts, transaction history
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, FCM tokens for push notifications

### Location Data
We collect precise location information to provide core delivery services:

- **Merchant Location**: Store/pickup address for order collection
- **Driver Location**: Real-time location tracking during active deliveries
- **Delivery Location**: Customer address for order delivery
- **Location Tracking**: Continuous location tracking for drivers during active orders only

**Important**: Location data is only collected when:
- You are logged in as a driver with an active order
- You have granted location permissions to the App
- Location tracking stops immediately when you go offline or complete your delivery

### Order and Usage Data
- Order details (items, addresses, prices, notes)
- Order status and history
- Communication records (in-app notifications, support requests)
- App usage statistics and crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Service Delivery
- Process and manage orders
- Connect merchants, drivers, and customers
- Provide real-time order tracking
- Calculate delivery fees and routes
- Send order status notifications

### Communication
- Send push notifications about order updates
- Provide customer support
- Send important service announcements
- Respond to your inquiries and requests

### Service Improvement
- Analyze app usage to improve functionality
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
- Protect our rights and property

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Information Sharing Within the App
- **Merchants** can see: Customer name, phone number, delivery address (when order is placed)
- **Drivers** can see: Merchant name, phone number, pickup address; Customer name, phone number, delivery address (when order is accepted)
- **Customers** can see: Driver name, phone number, real-time location (when order is in progress)

### Third-Party Service Providers
We share information with trusted third parties who help us operate the App:

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services (hosted on secure servers)
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notification delivery
- **Mapbox**: Map services and route calculation
- **WhatsApp**: Support communication (only when you initiate contact)

These providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Protect the safety of users or the public

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Authentication**: Secure phone-based authentication with OTP verification
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions ensure users only access appropriate data
- **Database Security**: Row-level security policies in our database
- **Regular Security Audits**: Ongoing monitoring for vulnerabilities

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

**Specific Retention Periods:**
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data retained for 2 years after last activity, then deleted
- **Order History**: Retained for 5 years for accounting and legal purposes
- **Location Data**: Real-time location data is not permanently stored; only current location during active deliveries

## Location Services

### Why We Collect Location Data
Location data is essential for:
- Connecting customers with nearby drivers
- Providing accurate delivery time estimates
- Enabling real-time order tracking
- Calculating fair delivery fees based on distance

### How to Control Location Sharing
- **Android**: Settings > Apps > Hur Delivery > Permissions > Location
- **iOS**: Settings > Hur Delivery > Location

**Note**: Disabling location services will prevent drivers from accepting and delivering orders.
